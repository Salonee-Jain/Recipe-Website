Healthy Rajasthani Recipes

Rajasthan has its own unique food, wonderful hospitality, colourful dresses and impressive historical monuments. Food is unique as in the past, water was very scarce there and most of dishes are made without using much green vegetables and such grains are used which could grow in desert. Still there are wholesome, nutritious and tasty. Best part is one dish is whole meal. Most of them are very easy to make and less time consuming.
