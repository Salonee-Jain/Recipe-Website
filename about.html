About The Authors

A recipe has no soul. You as the cook must bring soul to the recipe.




Ramita Mehta

Ramita currently lives in Mumbai. She got many opportunities to visit and live abroad. She believes that Travelling is a good way of learning many things from people in various countries. She has learned a lot from her extensive travels and from her experience in life. She is the fulltime director in her husband's company.She likes to participate and work in public life. She is associated with many NGOs. She is very fond of cooking new things. But always emphasizes that the food should be tasty as well as nutritious. She loves to do regular yoga, read books, work for NGOs. She is interested in storytelling and has recently started her own podcast named - nani dadi ki kahaniyan (stories from grandmothers). It can be found in Apple's App store or can be downloaded by stitcher.com on Android phones.



Rekha Khandelwal

Rekha lives in Mumbai. She loves meeting people and making friends. She is fond of sports and travelling. Rekha did her post-graduation in public administration from Rajasthan University after BSC. Rekha is very fond of cooking and learning different types of dishes. Her husband always encouraged her to cook and admired her cooking skills. She learned Rajasthani cooking from her mother. In the process of completing this book, she had the opportunity to learn a lot and I also had a lot of fun.
